What is Makeup?

Now what is makeup? In modern day, makeup is a broad name for the group of products that many people generally use on their face to enhance their features, create art, and express themselves. The usage of makeup is very popular amongst both women and men. Makeup dates back to 6000 BCE, where both female and male Egyptians would wear makeup. They believed it appealed to gods. Over time, this tradition of wearing makeup evolved over many different cultures, all until now. Where it is still commonly used for cultural reasons, or entertainment or for everyday wear. Here, you will find a variety of makeup products that are widely used.

Base Makeup

Base makeup is the base of everything as the name suggests. A face of makeup fully depends on the base. This can make or break anyone’s makeup look. Almost all makeup under the base makeup category goes on your entire face. It helps cover up dark spots, unwanted acne, evens out your skintone, and is the foundation for everything.

Eye Makeup

Eye makeup is one of the most unique parts of makeup. There are no rules to this, there is full freedom in it. Eye makeup catches anyone’s attention, whether bold, or natural. Many products are used to create many genres of makeup looks. Eye makeup revolves around the eyes as the name suggests. It comes in a variety of textures, colours, and formulations.

Lip Makeup

Lip Makeup is often used to enhance one's lip colours. Colours are very broad as there is no limit to makeup. Most often used though are reds, pinks, and peaches. They also come in many types of textures, and can be very moisturizing for the lips. Many different aesthetics can be achieved using the same products. Its the way you play around with these products that can help fit you and your wanted aesthetic.

Other Products and Info

Now there are products that don't necessarily fall under the categories listed above. They still are a major part to a makeup look. However, these may be less common and finding them may be more difficult. There are also many other factors that come with starting makeup and making it look good. Things like skincare, skin type, product ingredients also make a big difference.
